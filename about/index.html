About

Before that I was an undergrad at the University of California, Berkeley where I studied Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with a particular emphasis in Machine Learning and Optimization. I was also a MasterCard Foundation Scholar at Berkeley.

Even before that, I was born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria. I loved science subjects in secondary school and did A levels at Edgewood College. For a complete professional summary, you should check out my LinkedIn profile.

I like to write, upon reflection, and you can find a subset of the things I've written here. I also sometimes tweet shorter, less articulate things.
