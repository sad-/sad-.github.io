Here are a few projects I have worked on

  • Markov Music uses an empirical markov model to generate music similar to a set of input music files. Works best on non-verbal music like classical, maybe jazz.

  • IBM Smart Villages chatbot was a project I led for Machine Learning @ Berkeley to work on the IBM smart villages initiative to prototype a chatbot to empower people in remote parts in India to gain access to resources.

  • Multiagent reinforcement learning for HFO soccer used DQNs to train agents in the HFO Robocup soccer game simulation to learn play the ball forward and score goals. By tweaking reward scheme, I got the agents to also learn to collaborate by passing the ball, without explicitly rewarding collaboration.

  • Hierarchical Agglomerative Clustering was a study into how various linkage criteria and choice of metric functions affect the topological structures of phylogenetic tree estimates generated using agglomerative clustering.
